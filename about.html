About

X MPs is an independent project that documents Members of Parliament who receive income from X Corp, alongside a timestamped breakdown of declared payments.

The data is drawn from publicly available parliamentary declarations and is presented for transparency and research purposes.

Data sources

Sources and links used on the main page:

  • Register of Members' Financial Interests (UK Parliament)
  • TheyWorkForYou dataset mirror
  • Ofcom investigation announcement

How to use

Open the main page to see MPs with X-related declarations, ranked by total amount. Click an MP row to expand the list of individual payments.

Limitations

  • Declarations may lag behind real-world payments until they are published in the official register.
  • Payer names can vary (e.g. 'X Corp', 'X (formerly Twitter)'); matching relies on payer text patterns.
  • Figures shown are based on declared amounts and may not capture non-monetary benefits not listed in the dataset.

Contact

This is an independent transparency project. For questions about the underlying declarations, please refer to the official parliamentary register.
